Pro Tips for Success

Movement is Key: Always keep moving to avoid being surrounded by monsters. Use the entire play area to your advantage.

Prioritize Upgrades: Focus on collecting upgrades that complement your playstyle. Some upgrades provide offensive power, while others enhance defense or mobility.

Learn Monster Patterns: Different monsters have unique movement and attack patterns. Study them to anticipate their behavior.

Balance Risk and Reward: Sometimes you need to take calculated risks to collect valuable upgrades. Know when to be aggressive and when to play defensively.
